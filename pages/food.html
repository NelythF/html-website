Some of my favourite food:

  • Cachupa

    Cachupa is a Cape Verdean dish that my mom used to make, it's a very rich stew with beans, corns, different meats and fish.

  • Char Kuey Teow

    Char Kuey Teow is a stir fried noodle dish with shrimp and clams.

  • Nasi Lemak Rendang Daging

    Nasi Lemak is Malaysia's national dish of rice cooked with coconut milk, peanuts, sardines and sambal. Rendang Daging is a fragrant, delicious dry curry with tender beef.
